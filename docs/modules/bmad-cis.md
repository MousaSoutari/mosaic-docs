# bmad-cis

**BMAD Creative Intelligence Suite** -- innovation, design thinking, brainstorming, storytelling, and creative problem-solving with 6 specialized agents. Each agent brings a distinct creative methodology to your project.

Installed by: `npx @mousasoutari/mosaic-setup install --modules cis`

## Creative Methodologies

### Design Thinking
A human-centered approach to problem-solving with five phases: Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test. Maya guides you through empathy mapping, persona creation, "How Might We" statements, and rapid prototyping strategies.

### Innovation Strategy
Victor applies frameworks like the Business Model Canvas, Blue Ocean Strategy, and disruption theory to help you identify innovation opportunities, evaluate market gaps, and develop competitive positioning.

### Creative Problem Solving
Dr. Quinn combines TRIZ (Theory of Inventive Problem Solving), lateral thinking techniques, root cause analysis, and constraint-based creativity to tackle complex problems systematically rather than relying on inspiration alone.

### Storytelling
Sophia helps craft compelling narratives using story structures (three-act, hero's journey), emotional arcs, and audience analysis. Applicable to product pitches, brand narratives, user onboarding, and content strategy.

### Presentation Design
Caravaggio focuses on visual communication -- slide composition, data visualization, color theory, and presentation flow. Helps turn raw content into visually compelling presentations.

### Brainstorming
Carson facilitates structured brainstorming sessions with divergent thinking (quantity over quality), convergent thinking (evaluation and selection), and techniques like SCAMPER, mind mapping, and reverse brainstorming.
